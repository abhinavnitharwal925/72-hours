Chapter 05 — The Message

My phone changed without warning.

No sound. No vibration.

Just a screen I’d never seen before.

A QR code.

Under it:

Relocation access approved Report to: Santa Clara Civic Center — West Entrance Capacity: 9 72 hours

No explanation. No instructions.

Just a place and a number.

I turned my phone face down and walked out.
