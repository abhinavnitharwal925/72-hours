Chapter 09 — The First Split

A woman stepped forward with her husband and son.

Her phone showed two names.

The band snapped onto her wrist.

The guard stopped the boy.

She pulled him toward her. The guard held him back.

She kept saying his name.

Nothing changed.

Two names. Two bands. One person left outside.

That’s when people started counting on their fingers.

That’s when voices changed.
