Chapter 10 — Goodbye Without Words

When it was our turn, the QR scanned.

Bands snapped onto my parents’ wrists.

My mom looked at me like she was memorizing my face.

My dad nodded once.

My sister froze. My cousin held her.

No one said goodbye.

They couldn’t.

If they did, the crowd would hear it. If they did, someone would follow me. If they did, I wouldn’t leave.

I didn’t look back.

Because if I didn’t leave, I wouldn’t get to you.
