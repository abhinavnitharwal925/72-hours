Chapter 20 — Vanishing

We slowed near intake.

You and your family got out.

I stayed in the car.

Your mom thanked me again.

You turned back once.

I smiled.

“go,” I said.

You followed them.

Before you could turn around again, I stepped out.

Pulled my sleeve down.

Walked the other way.

Into the crowd.

Into the noise.

I didn’t look back.

I accepted it.

Because I did what I said I would do.

I came for you.

I got you there.

And if that’s where my story ends—

I’m okay with that.

Because loving you like this was never a mistake.

It was the point.
